# OFAC SDN Watch

## Get notified every time the US Treasury updates the OFAC sanctions list.

We compare the old and new SDN lists and tell you exactly what changed — new designations, removals, and modifications. No more manually downloading XML files and diffing them yourself.

**Essential for compliance teams, AML officers, and fintech developers.**

### What you get (free)

- **Weekly digest** of all SDN list changes
- **New designations** — who was added, what programs, what country
- **Removals** — who was delisted
- **Modifications** — name changes, program changes, alias updates
- **Plain-English summaries** — no XML parsing required

### How it works

1. We monitor the OFAC SDN list daily
2. When Treasury publishes an update, we diff the old and new lists automatically
3. You get a clear, readable summary delivered to your inbox

### Who is this for?

- **Compliance teams** who need to stay current on sanctions changes
- **AML/KYC engineers** building screening systems
- **Fintech developers** integrating sanctions checks
- **Legal teams** advising on international transactions
- **Anyone** required to screen against the OFAC SDN list
